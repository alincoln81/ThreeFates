Our Process

At Three Fates Digital, our process is meticulously designed to ensure the best results for our clients. Here's a brief overview of each step in our process:

Discovery

This initial phase is all about understanding your vision, goals, and requirements. We engage in in-depth discussions to gather insights about your business, target audience, and desired functionalities. This helps us create a tailored strategy that aligns with your objectives.

Proposal

Based on the information gathered during the discovery phase, we draft a comprehensive proposal. This document outlines the project scope, timeline, estimated budget, and a roadmap of how we plan to achieve your goals. It serves as a clear guide and agreement for both parties moving forward.

Design

Here, our creative team gets to work on the design. We focus on creating a user-friendly interface and an appealing aesthetic that reflects your brand identity. We ensure that the design is not only visually striking but also functional and easy to navigate.

Develop

Once the design is approved, our developers start building your website/application. This stage involves coding, creating content management systems, and integrating necessary functionalities. We prioritize clean, efficient code and responsive design to ensure the site/application works seamlessly across different devices and platforms.

Launch

After thorough testing and final approvals, we launch the website/application. This phase involves setting the site/application live, monitoring its performance, and making any necessary adjustments. We ensure a smooth transition, paying attention to aspects like SEO, load time, and user experience.
